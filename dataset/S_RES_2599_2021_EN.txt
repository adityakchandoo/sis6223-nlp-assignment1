               Resolution 2599 (2021)
               Adopted by the Security Council at its 8870th meeting, on
               30 September 2021

                    The Security Council,
                    Recalling its resolution 1970 (2011) and all its subsequent resolutions on Libya,
               including resolutions 2259 (2015), 2510 (2020), 2542 (2020) and 2570 (2021),
                    Recalling its determination in its resolution 2213 (2015) that the situation in
               Libya continues to constitute a threat to international peace and security,
                    1.   Decides to extend until 31 January 2022 the mandate of the United Nations
               Support Mission in Libya, as an integrated special political mission to carry out its
               mandate, as set out in resolution 2542 (2020) and paragraph 16 of resolution 2570
               (2021);
                    2.    Decides to remain actively seized of the matter.
