               Resolution 2638 (2022)
               Adopted by the Security Council at its 9073rd meeting, on
               22 June 2022

                    The Security Council,
                   Noting with regret the death of Judge Antônio Augusto Cançado Trindade on
               29 May 2022,
                     Noting further that a vacancy in the International Court of Justice for the
               remainder of the term of office of the deceased Judge has occurred and must be filled
               in accordance with the terms of the Statute of the Court,
                      Noting that, in accordance with article 14 of the Statute, the date of the election
               to fill the vacancy shall be fixed by the Security Council,
                      Decides that the election to fill the vacancy shall take place on 4 November
               2022 at a meeting of the Security Council and at a meeting of the General Assembly
               at its seventy-seventh session.
